About

I recently completed my Ph.D. in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at the University of California, Berkeley, where I studied lipid nanoparticles and protein coronas using label-free proteomics. I will soon begin work at Bain & Company, where I’m excited to apply a scientific problem-solving mindset to business strategy. I’ve always been interested in the intersection of science, innovation, and entrepreneurship, and I enjoy exploring how early-stage technologies translate into real-world impact.

Expertise: Drug delivery • Proteomics • Nanotechnology • Genetic engineering • Scientific writing • Market landscapes • Experimental design • Data analysis

Availability for ad-hoc projects is subject to current employer policies. Happy to discuss future collaborations.
