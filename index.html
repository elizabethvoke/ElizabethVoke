Ad-hoc

Consulting

I occasionally support founders and R&D leaders with focused, time-boxed projects. My edge combines hands-on LNP/proteomics expertise with structured, outcomes-oriented execution.

Typical engagements (1–4 weeks)

  • Landscape & signal scan: concise review of targets, modalities, comparators, and technical risks.
  • Assay & workflow design: label-free proteomics pipelines, corona isolation QC, and analysis plans.
  • Go/No-Go proof points: milestone framing, experiment prioritization, and readout criteria.
  • Data-to-insight: analysis notebooks (Python/pandas/matplotlib), figures, and decision memos.

Relevant experience

  • Ph.D., UC Berkeley (Landry Lab): LNP protein corona; density-gradient ultracentrifugation; LC-MS/MS.
  • Collaborations: Whitehead (MIT/CMU), Landry Lab, multi-site proteomics studies.
  • Fellowships: NDSEG; venture exposure via Northpond Ventures fellowship.
  • Consulting toolkit: structured problem-solving, crisp deliverables, stakeholder-ready slides.

Note: I’m currently at Bain; ad-hoc work considered after Bain or with appropriate approvals.
